Driver Drowsiness Alert Using Aurdino uno (Feb2024-Jun2024)
Drowsiness of the drivers is the main cause of accidents in the world. Due to lack of sleep and tiredness, drowsiness can occur while driving. Eye blinking is considered as important evidence of driver drowsiness, a portable and low-cost device for monitoring a driver's drowsiness is proposed. The proposed system consists of detect eye blinking based on IR sensors mounted on eyewear. The detected prolonged blinking is used to trigger an audio alarm system which draws the driver's attention back. By this product occurrence of accidents has been reduced in thisway.
